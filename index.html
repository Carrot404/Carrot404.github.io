Introduction

I'm currently a Ph.D. candidate in the Robotics, Vision & Control Lab in the ZJU-UIUC Institute at Zhejiang University, Haining, China, supervised by Professor Liangjing Yang. Before that, I received my B. Eng. degree in Mechanical Engineering from Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics, Nanjing, China, in 2019.

Research Interest

My research interests include medical robotics and medical image processing. I am currently working on Ultrasound Image-Guided Collaborative Robot.
